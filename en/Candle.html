Aroma Candle

Project information

  • Category: Workshop
  • Position: Holder
  • Project date: 3/2023

My friends and I collaborated to plan and organize an aromatherapy candle workshop. The main objective of this project was to foster interaction and connection among individuals who enjoy DIY and creative activities. We had 10 participants and a team of 4 members. Together, we worked on every stage of the project, from designing and creating labels and posters to preparing materials and developing teaching materials.

The most challenging aspect of the project was managing the budget and securing a suitable venue. While it was difficult to keep costs low, we gained valuable insights and knowledge through this experience. We explored innovative solutions and practiced effective resource management despite the budget constraints. From this experience, I learned the importance of cost management and identified areas for improvement. Moving forward, I am committed to researching strategies for more efficient cost management in future projects and striving for further growth and improvement.
