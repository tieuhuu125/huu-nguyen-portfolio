クローシェ

Project information

  • Category: Workshop
  • Position: Holder
  • Project date: 4/2023

The purpose of this project is to provide a platform for individuals who enjoy handicrafts and crafts to come together, learn crochet techniques, and engage in meaningful interactions. With 10 participants and a team of 4 members, we collaborated on every aspect of the event, from planning to preparation and execution.

Throughout this project, we gained valuable insights and experiences in teamwork and event management. Interacting with the participants highlighted the importance of sharing information and ideas among craft enthusiasts, as well as the mutual inspiration and support that can arise from such interactions.
