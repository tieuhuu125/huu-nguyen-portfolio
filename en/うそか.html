In today's society, people have become quick to judge others. One day, Naoya Nakajima (21 years old) saw a red mark on the neck of his crush, Kajun Takeshima (21 years old), and was told it was a mosquito bite. But is that really true?

In modern society, individuals often make snap judgments about others. However, I cannot determine whether the claim of Naoya Nakajima (21 years old), who saw a red mark on the neck of his crush, Kajun Takeshima (21 years old), and was told it was a mosquito bite, is true or not. In such situations, it is important to verify the truth by directly asking the person involved, or by gathering additional information and evidence. When assessing individuals, it is crucial to approach the situation with fairness and objectivity.
