Umeboshi

Project information

  • Category: Short Documentary
  • Position: Director
  • Project date: 2/2022
  • Project URL: https://youtu.be/x2cxaPihQmQ

During my solo visit alone to Tanabe and Minabe towns in Wakayama Prefecture, known as the land of plums, I had the opportunity to learn about umeboshi (pickled plums) from the experts. The plum farmers in this region face challenges such as labor shortage and succession issues, and some have employed Vietnamese trainees to help with their operations. Through the art of making umeboshi, I aim to showcase Japanese culinary culture and traditional values to the Vietnamese community, bridging the gap between our cultures.
